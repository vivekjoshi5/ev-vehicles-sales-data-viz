Introduction

Electronic Vehicles or EVs as they are commonly addressed as, are an environment friendly invention in the history of mankind that could be a game changer in the impact of carbon on the environment, reducing the carbon footprints per person to a great extent. The data used in this project is a very simple table that shows market sales & market share of EVs produced by countries & major regions investing highly in EVs production. The figures are from the year 2021.

Objective

To clean data & visualize data to show highest & lowest sales figures and market share figures of ev vehicles among different regions.

Data

The data that is used for this project will be imported from this page https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electric_car_use_by_country

Methodologies

I will be using Spreadsheet software Microsoft Excel for the entire process.

Data Importing

Using the Import from web tool in excel, I imported one of the Tables from the data source. The data contains 13rows & 5 columns.

Data Cleaning

After importing this is how the table looks inside excel. -The data seems to be in text form instead of numerical. This can be checked using TYPE function. -The textual data can be converted into numerical data by multiplying with 1. -There seems to be two column headers. One of which can be removed. -The row 16 contains textual information which can be removed.

Data Exploration

The Columns that are not required have been dropped. The data is cleaned now and ready for Exploration & visualization. The data contains Sales in numbers and market share in percentage. I will use Excel’s Charts & Graphs tool to visualize and analyze the data showing different countries on x axis and the sales numbers & market share on y axis.

Data Communication

The charts show the sales & market share of different regions during the year 2021. Findings from the charts are as follows:

  • The maximum number of Electronic Vehicles has been sold by China followed by Europe.
  • Although China holds the top position in EV Sales, it's market share is less as compared to other larger regions.
  • Norway has largest market share of Electronic vehicles followed by Sweden. In Norway more number of people prefer EVs over Non-EV vehicles.
